Privacy Policy

How Dishlo collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: May 7, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 7, 2026

Dishlo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Dishlo mobile application (the "App"), how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

By using Dishlo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and the password you provide (stored securely via Firebase Authentication — we never see your raw password).
  • Profile Information: You may optionally provide a display name or full name.
  • App Content: Recipes, meal plans, grocery lists, and other content you create within the App are stored on our servers in order to provide the core functionality of the App and to sync your data across devices.
  • Usage Data: We may collect basic information about how you interact with the App (such as feature usage patterns) to improve the App experience. This data is not linked to personally identifiable information.
  • Device Information: We may collect general device and platform information (operating system, device type) for the purpose of diagnosing errors and improving App compatibility.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your precise or approximate location.
  • We do not collect financial information or payment data.
  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers.
  • We do not use your data to serve advertisements.
  • We do not collect information from your camera roll beyond images you explicitly upload within the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Dishlo App and its features.
  • Authenticate your identity and keep your account secure.
  • Sync your recipes, meal plans, and grocery lists across your devices.
  • Process images and text you upload to extract recipe information using AI services.
  • Generate AI-produced concept images for recipes you save.
  • Estimate nutritional information for your recipes.
  • Send optional push notifications (such as reminders to save recipes you've discovered). You can disable these at any time in your device settings.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve App performance.

4. Third-Party Services

Dishlo uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Used for user authentication, cloud data storage (Firestore), and backend infrastructure. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI / Google Gemini: Used to parse recipe text and images, generate recipe concept images, and estimate nutritional content. Images and text you upload may be processed by these services. We do not share identifying personal information with these services beyond the content you explicitly submit for processing.
  • Expo (by Expo Inc.): Used to build and deliver the App on iOS and Android. See Expo Privacy Policy.

We carefully select third-party services and only share the minimum data necessary to provide the App's functionality.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers provided by Google Firebase. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your recipes, meal plans, and grocery lists are also cached locally on your device (using SQLite) so that the App functions when you are offline.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and content data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services. Locally cached data older than 14 days is automatically cleaned up on your device. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us (see Section 9).

7. Children's Privacy

Dishlo is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove that information.

8. Push Notifications

Dishlo may request permission to send you push notifications, such as reminders to save a recipe you've found. You can opt out of notifications at any time through your device's notification settings. Revoking notification permission does not affect your ability to use any other feature of the App.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional features such as push notifications.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@dishlo.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
