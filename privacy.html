Privacy Policy

How Dishlo collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Dishlo ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application that helps you save recipes, plan meals, and generate grocery lists. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By creating an account and using Dishlo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used to identify your account and send verification and password-reset emails
  • Full name — used to personalize your experience

2.2 Content You Create

All content you create inside Dishlo is stored and associated with your account:

  • Recipes — names, ingredients, instructions, serving sizes, recipe type, nutrition information, source URLs (if imported from the web), and AI-generated food images
  • Meal plans — dates, meal times (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack), and the recipes assigned to each slot
  • Grocery lists — items, quantities, units, categories, and check-off status
  • Recipe tags — labels you create to organize your recipes
  • Your Items — a personal catalog of custom grocery items you've added

2.3 Uploaded Files

If you use the scan-a-recipe feature, you may upload photos or PDF files of physical recipes. These files are temporarily processed to perform text extraction and are not retained beyond that process.

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or behavioral analytics beyond what is necessary to operate the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide Dishlo's features:

  • To operate your account — authenticate you, store your data, and sync it across your devices
  • To parse recipes — when you add a recipe by pasting text or a URL, we send that content to OpenAI's API to extract structured recipe data (ingredients, instructions, servings, etc.)
  • To generate recipe images — we send a description of your recipe to Google's Gemini API to generate a representative food photo
  • To extract text from photos — when you photograph a recipe, we send the image to Google Cloud Vision to read the text
  • To generate grocery lists — we aggregate ingredients from your meal plan entirely within our own servers; no AI is involved in this process
  • To send transactional emails — verification emails and password-reset links via Firebase

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Dishlo uses the following third-party services to function. Each has its own privacy policy:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Google Firebase Authentication, database (Firestore), and file storage firebase.google.com/support/privacy
OpenAI Recipe text parsing openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
Google Gemini Recipe image generation policies.google.com/privacy
Google Cloud Vision OCR text extraction from photos and PDFs policies.google.com/privacy

When you use features that involve these services, relevant data (recipe text, image descriptions, or uploaded photos) is transmitted to them. We do not share your account information (name or email) with any of these providers.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your recipes, meal plans, grocery lists, and profile are stored in Google Firestore, hosted in Google Cloud.
  • A local copy of your data is also cached on your device (using SQLite and device storage) to allow offline use. This data is private to your device and your account.
  • Uploaded photos used for OCR are temporarily stored in Google Cloud Storage and deleted after text extraction is complete.
  • AI-generated recipe images are stored in Google Cloud Storage and linked to your recipe.
  • All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Access to your data is restricted to your account only. No other Dishlo user can see your recipes, meal plans, or grocery lists.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete individual recipes, meal plans, and grocery lists at any time from within the app.

To request deletion of your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email address listed in Section 10. We will process account deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Dishlo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information (you can update your name in the app's Account settings)
  • Delete your data (see Section 6)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 10.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will notify you within the app. Continued use of Dishlo after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
