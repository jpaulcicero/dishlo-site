The Flow

Everything from recipe to table,
in five simple steps.

Dishlo is built around one simple workflow: save recipes, plan meals, generate a grocery list from your planned meals, shop, and cook with helpful tools.

01
📸
Save

Add recipes from screenshots, photos, pasted recipe text, or PDFs. Dishlo extracts ingredients, instructions, servings, and estimates nutrition.

02
📅
Plan

Assign recipes to days and meal times on your rolling home calendar. Breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack — set the date, slot, and servings.

03
🛒
Generate

Pick a date range and tap Generate List. Dishlo combines ingredients from every planned meal in that range into one organized grocery list.

04
🏪
Shop

Open Shop Mode for a large, easy-to-read checklist. Tap items as you shop, adjust font size, copy items, and keep your screen awake mid-aisle.

05
🍳
Cook

Follow ingredients and instructions in Cook Mode with timers, a unit converter, servings adjustment, and side scroll controls for hands-free navigation.

Features

Built for real cooks,
real families, real life.

Every feature in Dishlo exists to reduce friction between a recipe you want to save and the meal you want to put on the table.

📖
Recipe Library

Your personal recipe collection, always with you. Search, filter, tag, edit, and open recipes when you need them.

Add from a screenshot, photo, pasted recipe text, or PDF
Review extracted text before saving
AI-generated concept image for every recipe
Estimated nutrition per serving
Tag and filter recipes your way
📅
Meal Planning

A rolling meal calendar centered around today, with quick access to your planned meals.

Assign recipes to Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snack
Set servings per planned meal
Expand one day or all days at once
View, cook, or remove planned meals from Quick Actions
🛒
Smart Grocery Lists

Choose a date range, tap Generate, and your grocery list builds itself from your planned meals.

Ingredients automatically combined and categorized
Pantry Check hides items you already have
Included Meals shows which recipes built the list
Add extras or one-off items at any time
Past grocery lists are saved automatically
🏪
Shop Mode

A distraction-free shopping view designed to be used with one hand mid-aisle.

Large, legible text with S, M, L, XL font sizes
Tap items to check them off as you shop
Screen stay-awake is on by default
Hide checked items or reset the session
Copy the full list or tap an item name to copy it
🍳
Cook Mode

A hands-free cooking companion that keeps your recipe readable while your hands are busy.

Ingredients and instructions in separate tabs
Side scroll controls for swipe-free navigation
Sugar spoon tools drawer with timer and unit converter
Servings adjuster for scaling recipes
Status ribbon for timer and servings changes
🔄
Your Items & Sync

Manage frequently used grocery items and keep your recipes, plans, and lists available across sessions.

Your Items stores staples and frequently added groceries
Offline-first updates save locally first
Changes sync automatically when you reconnect
Secure account with email and password

Helpful tools, with clear responsibility.

Dishlo is designed as a personal recipe organization and meal planning tool. Dishlo does not scrape websites or fetch recipes from URLs. When you save a recipe, you provide the content yourself by uploading a screenshot, photo, PDF, or by pasting the actual recipe text.

Source URLs are saved only as references so you can remember where a recipe came from. AI-generated recipe images are original concept images meant to be visual anchors; they may not perfectly match the final dish.

Nutrition estimates are general approximations only and are not intended for medical, dietary, allergy, or health decisions. Always verify ingredients, allergens, cooking instructions, and food safety details before preparing or serving food.
