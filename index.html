The Flow

Everything from recipe to table,
in five simple steps.

Dishlo is built around a single, seamless workflow. Whether you're saving a recipe from a cookbook or building a week of meals for your family, the path is always the same.

01
📸
Save

Capture any recipe — from a screenshot, a photo of a cookbook page, pasted text, or a PDF. Dishlo extracts ingredients, instructions, and servings automatically.

02
📅
Plan

Assign recipes to days and meal times on your rolling home calendar. Breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack — plan as far ahead as you like.

03
🛒
Generate

Pick a date range and tap Generate List. Dishlo combines every ingredient from your planned meals into a single, organized grocery list.

04
🏪
Shop

Open Shop Mode for a large, easy-to-read list. Tap items to check them off aisle by aisle. The screen stays awake so you never have to unlock mid-shop.

05
🍳
Cook

Follow step-by-step instructions in Cook Mode with built-in timers, a unit converter, and a servings scaler — all without touching the screen between steps.

Features

Built for real cooks,
real families, real life.

Every feature in Dishlo exists to reduce friction between a great recipe and a great meal.

📖
Recipe Library

Your personal recipe collection, always with you. Search, filter by type, and browse alphabetically.

Import from a screenshot, photo, or PDF — Dishlo reads it
Paste text from anywhere and Dishlo parses it instantly
AI-generated concept image for every recipe
Estimated nutrition per serving (protein, carbs, fat, calories)
Tag and filter recipes your way
📅
Meal Planning

A rolling weekly calendar right on your home screen. Meal planning that actually stays up to date.

Assign any recipe to Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snack
Set servings per planned meal
Quick actions to cook, edit, or remove any planned meal
Visual calendar so the week is always in view
🛒
Smart Grocery Lists

Choose a date range, tap Generate, and your grocery list builds itself from every planned meal.

Ingredients automatically combined and categorized
Pantry Check — hide items you already have at home
See which meals contributed to each item
Add extras or one-off items at any time
Drag-and-drop category reordering for your store's layout
🏪
Shop Mode

A distraction-free shopping interface designed to be used with one hand mid-aisle.

Large, legible text — adjustable S, M, L, XL font sizes
Tap to check off items as you go
Screen stay-awake — no unlocking between aisles
Checked items visually separate from what's left
🍳
Cook Mode

A hands-free cooking companion that keeps your screen readable while your hands are full.

Step-by-step instructions with one-tap navigation
Side-arrow scrolling — swipe-free between steps
Built-in timer with presets and custom durations
Unit converter — no more mental math mid-recipe
Live servings scaler adjusts ingredient amounts
🔄
Your Items & Sync

Manage a persistent pantry list and keep everything synced across devices.

Your Items — a running list of staples and pantry inventory
Automatic background sync when you come online
Offline-first — works without a connection
Secure account with email & password
