Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your Dishlo account and data.

Option 1 — Delete in the App (instant)

Open Dishlo and go to Account → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Option 2 — Delete by Email

If you no longer have the app installed, email us at dishlo.app@gmail.com from the email address on your account with the subject "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

Account deletion permanently removes:

  • Your profile (name and email address)
  • All recipes, including AI-generated recipe images
  • All meal plans
  • All grocery lists and Your Items
  • All recipe tags

What May Be Retained

Subscription and transaction records held by the Apple App Store, Google Play, and our subscription processor may be retained as required for financial reporting, fraud prevention, and legal compliance. Dishlo never has your payment card details.

IMPORTANT: DELETING YOUR ACCOUNT DOES NOT CANCEL AN ACTIVE SUBSCRIPTION. CANCEL YOUR SUBSCRIPTION IN YOUR APP STORE'S SUBSCRIPTION SETTINGS BEFORE OR AFTER DELETING YOUR ACCOUNT TO AVOID FUTURE CHARGES.

How to cancel: Google Play — Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions. Apple — Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

Questions? Contact us at dishlo.app@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
